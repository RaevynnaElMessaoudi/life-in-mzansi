Welcome to Mzansi

Origin of Mzansi
From Xhosa uMzantsi, from um- (singular prefix) + -zantsi ‘low, south’.
Meaning: South Africa
Life in Mzansi's goal is to introduce people around the world to South Africa
Our hope is to achieve this by focusing on the core aspects that make up life in South Africa,
Mainly it's unique geography, its melting pot of diverse cultures, its famous wildlife and its unique history
